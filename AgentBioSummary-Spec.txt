This is a high level spec that describes the AgentBioSummary system.

The AgentBioSummary system is a agentic system that will search the web
for the most relevant articles in synthetic biology, create a summary of 
a new articles from the past 24 hours, and then create a summary of the 
the top 10 articles. The summary should be understandable for a 15 year 
old high school student, who has a good foundation is basic biology. 

The agent will run every day at a certain time and the summary will be 
emailed to a recipient with an email address.
